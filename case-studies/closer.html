Art Institute of Chicago: Closer

The Art Institute of Chicago has thousands of works of art on viewâ€”each with their own stories to tell. The AIC hired the IMA Lab to develop an iOS mobile application, Closer, to help bring these stories to life. The app was built on the TAP platform. The initial result is an elegant interface highlighting ten artworks in the museum's renowned collection of modern art and eventually encompassing other collections. Each artwork includes a range of multimedia materials including zoomable imagery, object information, multi-page stories, and share features. Closer provides an informative and engaging experience for visitors while offering a flexible design for expanding content.
